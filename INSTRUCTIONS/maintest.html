INSTRUCTIONS TO BE FOLLOWED WHILE TAKING UP THE TEST



1. Once you go to EXAM CORNER,you will observe 2 frames. On the left frame, we have your monitoring process and on the right side we have your test . On the left frame,enter your subject code and your USN number and then click START REC button for the screen recording process to begin.

2. On the right frame,enter your subject code and your lecturer's name in order to obtain your text link.

3. Once you obtain the test link,start answering and once you finish your test,click submit button.

4. After submitting the test , you can click STOP REC button and the screen recording process will cease. And you will have your video downloaded in your DOWNLOADS section. Now choose that file and calculate its CHECKSUM value.

5. After submitting your test, you will be redirected to a page where you will have to upload your video link(upload the video to your drive and share that link) and provide the CHECKSUM value along with your name and USN.

6. After providing the necessary details , you will be redirected to the HOME PAGE.
